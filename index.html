Frequently Asked Questions

What is a payment gateway?
A payment gateway securely processes transactions between customers, merchants, and banks.
How does a payment gateway work?
It encrypts payment details and routes them to banks for approval.
Are payment gateways secure?
Yes, they use encryption, tokenization, and fraud detection systems.
How can I track my transactions?
Through real-time dashboards and transaction reports.
What currencies are supported?
Multiple global currencies including INR, USD, EUR, and more.
